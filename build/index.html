About Me

I’m a multidiscipline front-end developer with extensive experience in HTML, CSS, CraftCMS, ExpressionEngine, JavaScript and E-Commerce development. I enjoy working as a team with product owners, stakeholders and creatives to bring ideas to life, produce results and ongoing product manageability.

I’ve put the E-Commerce site on Craft CMS to manage the content and using HTML/CSS, JavaScript I’ve been able to eliminate much of the jQuery and help reduce overall code-bloat. The project has been a huge overtaking but incredibly rewarding as I get to wear many hats and continually see results.
